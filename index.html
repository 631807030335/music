Mainland Music History

Chinese music can be traced back to 9,000 years ago. The Jiahu bone flute unearthed at the Jiahu site is about 9,000 to 7,500 years ago. About 7000 years ago, bone whistles and primitive pottery xuns began to appear everywhere. Ge Tian's "three people fucking oxtails, cast enough to sing eight songs", it can be seen that ancient music culture has the characteristics of combining song, dance and music.

Western Music History

Western music has its initial form since ancient Greece and Rome. After the Middle Ages, it was based on people's belief in gods and laid a good foundation for the development of Western music in the future. In the Baroque period, due to the improvement of musical rhythm and the transition of music from church to society, Western music began its heyday. Musicians such as Beethoven and Mozart in the classical period made great contributions to the development of Western music. In the 19th century, Western music began to express personal emotions, making the style of music more soft and warm. After the 20th century, many styles, genres and types of music appeared, which made the classification of music more comprehensive and rich.

The gap between Chinese and Western music

Chinese music documents generally date back to the time of the Yellow Emperor. According to archaeological discoveries, Chinese music can be traced back to more than 7,000 years ago. The Chinese nation has created a rich music culture over thousands of years of history. Chinese music has had a profound influence on the music of China's surrounding areas. Western music actually started from the end of the 16th century, especially from the 17th to the 18th century. This period is called the Baroque period in the history of music. Western classical music has theories, standards, and a large number of text and sound documents, which are easy to circulate; it emphasizes aesthetics and functionality, so it is relatively objective.
